Ukraine is a country in Eastern Europe. It is the second-largest European country. It has a population of around 40 million people. It is bordered by Russia to the east and northeast; by Belarus to the north; by Poland, Slovakia, and Hungary to the west; and by Romania and Moldova to the southwest; with a coastline along the Black Sea and the Sea of Azov to the south and southeast, respectively. Kyiv is Ukraine capital as well as its largest city. The country language is Ukrainian, and many people are also fluent in Russian.
